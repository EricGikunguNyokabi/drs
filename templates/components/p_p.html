Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our document retrieval system. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data in compliance with the Kenyan Data Protection Act.

  • Personal Identifiers: Including but not limited to your name, address, email address, phone number, and identification documents.
  • Transactional Information: Such as records of documents requested and retrieved, payment information, and user interactions with our platform.
  • Device Information: Including IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and other technical data collected automatically when you access our platform.

Use of Personal Information

  • Document Retrieval Services: To facilitate the retrieval and delivery of requested documents to users.
  • Account Management: To manage user accounts, provide customer support, and communicate with users regarding their accounts.
  • Improving Services: To analyze user behavior and preferences, personalize user experience, and enhance the functionality of our platform.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, including responding to lawful requests and protecting our rights and interests.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We restrict access to personal information to authorized personnel only and regularly review our security practices to ensure compliance with industry standards.

Third-Party Services

We may engage third-party service providers to assist with the operation of our platform or provide services on our behalf. These third parties may have access to your personal information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
